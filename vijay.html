Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy," is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, a character from the One Piece manga and anime series. He dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates by finding the legendary One Piece treasure. Luffy is known for his flexible rubber body, gained from eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi, a Devil Fruit. He's a fearless and cheerful character, often prioritizing friendship and adventure over personal gain. Luffy's journey involves battling powerful enemies, both pirates and the World Government, and establishing alliances with various individuals and groups. He is also a skilled fighter who uses his rubber abilities to create a variety of powerful attacks.
